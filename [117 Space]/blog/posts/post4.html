This weeks readings covered chapters 14 through 17 of the text, which focused on CSS and various layout techniques. While a lot of the content was familiar to me, it was helpful getting to review its specific properties, and understanding all the possible values they can hold in more detail.

One point I found particularly interesting was the use of the circle() notation to create a shape for text to wrap around. I was aware that text could wrap around rectangular images, however, I didn't know it could follow elliptical/shaped patterns as well.
