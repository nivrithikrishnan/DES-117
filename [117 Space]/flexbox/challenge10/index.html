Flexbox Challege 10

Turn the section into a flexbox and then turn the articles inside the section into flexboxes. Set the flex-direction property for the articles to column so that you can align the anchor tags to the bottom center of the articles.

There are at least two ways to achive the bottom center alignment of the buttons. One way is to use margin with auto properties, and the other is to use a combination of margin with an auto property set and the align-self property.
